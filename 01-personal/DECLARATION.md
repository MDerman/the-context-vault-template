# 01-personal Declaration

## Identity

### Constitution

Create every day. Teach everything you know. Work in public.

Keep a promise or a principle to yourself. That is the base layer. Not a mood. Not a productivity trick. A standard.

I only do and believe that which moves me toward my goals.

The point is agency: time, location, wealth, health, love, creativity, and enough freedom to build a life that feels chosen.

### Standards

- Protect attention like it is the business.
- Calendarize as much as reasonable because it is better.
- Move daily. Train 4x weekly when the body allows.
- Be bored. Walk. Meditate. Twiddle thumbs.
- Use pen and paper when the head is noisy.
- Operate from joy, gratitude, and enlightenment; not shame.

## Momentum

### Rhythm

Use:

- Daily note for highlight, daily Number 1s etc. and evening review;
- Weekly note for next-week planning and doing calendar blocking;
- Quarterly note for WIG, lead/lag measures, top goals, and start/stop/keep;
- yearly note for identity, values, dreamline, assets, and systems.

Monthly periodic notes are intentionally not used.

### Work Cadence

- 6am to 6pm is the target daily work container.
- 1:2 split across marketing/outreach days and coding/building days. 7 days of marketing/outreach, 14 days of coding. repeat.
- 3x 4 hours work blocks per day is the goal. Mornings for long-term/marketing etc, middle block for urgent/important, 3rd block for maintenance/learning.
- Marketing days: talk/DM 30+ people, write, build lead magnets, or ship public work.
- Coding days: marketing in the morning if needed, then build.
- Coding feels productive by default. Launches, lead magnets, viral videos, waitlists, and outreach need deliberate protection.

### Focus and Rules

- 📅 Calendar block. Important things first. Delay the non-essential as long as possible.
- IF you can do it in 15 minutes, do it now.
- 📝 Pen and paper > tech.
- 📵 Open phone only when seriously needed. Less music in car, no phone when walking, going to kitchen bathroom etc.
- 🧘 Stare at wall for 10 mins or do NSDR when you don’t feel like working.
- ⏲️ Set timers for work block - beat the time! See how much you actually work. Pause when not working.
- Inhabit the in-between
