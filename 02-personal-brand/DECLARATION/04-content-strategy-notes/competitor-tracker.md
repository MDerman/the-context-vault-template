## Market Gaps

- Most tools help people publish more, but not necessarily sound more specific.
- Most AI writing tools do not understand the business deeply enough.
- Many LinkedIn products separate ideas, writing, scheduling, commenting, analytics, and GTM context.
- Agencies can be expensive and may still fail to capture the founder's real point of view.

## Claims to Avoid

- Fully automated thought leadership.
- Generic "AI writes your posts" promises.
- Replacing taste, thinking, or customer understanding.
- Old product names.

## Claims I Can Own

- Building Claude Code for LinkedIn GTM.
- Tasteful AI-assisted content and social selling.
- Founder-led LinkedIn GTM.
- Real audience language turned into content, conversations, and product learning.
