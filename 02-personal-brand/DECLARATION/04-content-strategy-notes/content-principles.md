# Content Principles

Principles to keep close when writing posts and designing content systems.

> I want to write refreshing, thought-provoking but highly actionable ideas & stories that leave people empowered to build their business / startup via their own personal brand.

> Focus on specificity to establish authority.

> Align content strategy with software offering to create a feedback loop.

> Incorporate more polarizing content.

> People learn from guides, not gurus.

> demonstrate expertise that people are willing to pay for.

> Find strong opinions / niche knowledge / what interests and surprises you.

> You curate information for them.

> You share 'novel ideas'.

> People don't have short attention spans... They have short consideration spans.

> slowing down is when creativity begins to emerge.
