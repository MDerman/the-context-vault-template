# Content Franchises

Recurring formats make publishing easier and make my brand easier to remember.

## Content Pillars

| Pillar | Audience Problem | Promise | Example Topics | Repeatable Series or Franchise |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| LinkedIn copywriting and social selling | I post but it does not create business. | Turn LinkedIn into a GTM motion. | Hooks, comments, profile conversion, DMs, lead magnets, inbound leads. | LinkedIn GTM teardown. |
| AI automation for inbound leads on LinkedIn | I want leverage without generic AI output. | Use AI to support taste, not replace it. | AI workflows, prompt systems, audience language, content ops. | AI slop vs tasteful workflow. |
| Founder journey and product building | I want to trust the person building the tool. | Make the build visible and useful. | Impression lessons, customer calls, product decisions, mistakes. | What I learned building Impression this week. |
| Personal brand strategy | I do not know what to be known for. | Clarify audience, proof, positioning, authority, and content pillars. | One true fan, niche, story, proof, strong beliefs. | Strong belief post. |
| Writing as thinking | I need clearer ideas, not just more posts. | Use writing to think, teach, and build trust. | Essays, notes, idea capture, narrative, useful stories. | Build-in-public product note. |
