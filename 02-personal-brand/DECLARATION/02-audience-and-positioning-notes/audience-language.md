# Audience Language

Audience language to keep close when writing posts, offers, profile copy, and product pages.

## Current Audience

> NOW: early-stage founders who want to grow their linkedin audience and business with inbound leads on linkedin.

> I write for aspiring or early phase founders, entrepreneurs and creators.

> My target audience is teachable, values outside input and has a passion for learning.

> I write for people who prioritize their health and well-being and are super goal driven.

> I write for people who seek to be famous and successful, making an impact comes second.

> If you're here it means you are ambitious, have an open mind, have a desire for greater impact, meaning and purpose in your life, and are willing to take risks.

> But of course, the greatest risk is taking no risk at all.

## What I Hear Them Thinking and Feeling

> I want to be famous and earn a decent living online.

> I don't know what to write or make videos about.

> I'm so exhausted and can't focus and feel so overwhelmed and defeated by this wild west that is the internet.

> I know I will eventually succeed because I will never stop working. There is no other alternative.

> I see how everyone on the internet is manipulating and don't think it's actually a bad thing - I want to make the same performance they do.

## Transformation

> Large social following, consistent income & confidence they have brought something of meaning and value to the world.

> I am speaking to freedom of choice, authentic self-expression, recognition/impact, legacy, financial security, confidence, community, control over time, purpose.
