# Proof and Credibility

Why this brand is earned instead of theoretical.

## Builder Proof

> I've always been a builder.

> When it's time to build something I become obsessed.

> I'm incredibly proud of what I've built now- I use it every time I post on LinkedIn.

> The point is - that my idea sprung out of the problems I experienced - being the market itself!

> I just have to set my focus on one core product and audience at first.

> It's too difficult to build AND build an audience on multiple platforms.

## Internet Proof

> I knew absolutely nothing about animation or 3d modelling or video editing.

> I had utter conviction in my success, despite any proof of my ability.

> social media lead me to health & calisthenics coaches which transformed my entire life.

> social media introduced me to fitness which indirectly solved so many problems in my life.

## Hard Season Proof

> I was working blind on what I thought were the most important things, with no paying customers and running on savings.

> The hardest season was this last winter before my first paying users.

> Felt trapped physically in our tiny cold apartment and with the business.

> It felt like every day I stumbled across another competitor app.

## Lessons Earned

> I've become a lot better at assessing software/architecting software.

> I've learnt that everyone has a story to tell.

> I wish I knew that you've got to be SPECIFIC.

> One Solution. One Person. One Service (or product).

> A real business isn't some vague beautiful idea. It solves a real fking problem.
