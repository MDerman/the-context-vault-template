## The Tinder Test (Round 2)

Now that you’ve thought things through, let’s write our purpose statement again:

<aside>
<img src="https://www.notion.so/icons/rename_blue.svg" alt="https://www.notion.so/icons/rename_blue.svg" width="40px" /> **Write your answer to the Tinder Test below**

**He says you combine 3 things:**

1. Your avatar + what they get
2. Your unique method
3. Your social proof

**Avatar + what they get:**

I help [type of person] who wants to achieve [desired transformation] by sharing [your method].

**Unique method:**

Your unique method is:

- How you approach problems
- How you communicate
- Your values
- Core themes you come back to

Brainstorm by reviewing your experience, work style, writing style, interests, passions, and purpose.

**Social Proof:**

This answers the question “why should we trust you”?

Use one or several of these types of social proof:

- company name
- years of experience
- dollar value made / supported
- number of people / companies supported
- followers on other social platform
- category of company type
- testimonial / results
- email subs
- ambitious goal
- building in public

Put those 3 things together and you have your niche.
