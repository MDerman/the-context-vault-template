# Profile checklist

## **1. Profile**

### **Essentials**

- **Profile Picture**
    
    *Professional headshot with a smile*
    
- **Banner**
    
    *Challenging question related to your offer*
    
- **Headline**
    
    *Who you are, what you do, how you help*
    

---

### **Featured Section**

Create one-click links to convert leads:

- **→ Premium Offer**
    
    *Calendly link with qualifying questions*
    
- **→ Lower-Ticket Offers**
    
    *Digital product or way to engage before committing*
    

---

### **About Section**

Polish it to speak *directly* to your target audience:

- **→ Target your buyer persona**
    
    *Use their words, examples, and show you understand their problems*
    
- **→ Present your service as the solution**
    
    *Clearly explain how it helps improve their life*
    
- **→ Set expectations**
    
    *Briefly outline results, process, and methods used*
    

---

### **Build Trust**

- **→ Experience**
    
    *Evidence of past work, qualifications, and client testimonials (link to them if possible)*
    
- **→ Recommendations**
    
    *Ask for testimonials from previous clients or colleagues, ideally from related industries*
    

---

### **Branding & Conversion Tweaks**

- **→ Consistent Colors/Branding**
    
    *Choose colors and design that reflect your business and persona*
    
- **→ Smart Link Usage**
    
    *Make the sales process easy with clear CTAs and Calendly links*
