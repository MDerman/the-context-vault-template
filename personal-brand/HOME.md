## Routing Rules

- Use `_obsidian/content` when the item needs content frontmatter, status, publication, platform, or publish-date tracking.
- Use root folders for easy-to-edit operating docs and assets that should not be buried in the content pipeline.
- Use `Lead Magnets` for opt-ins even when they support blogs, newsletters, or social posts.
- Use `_obsidian/tasks` only for executable next actions, reminders, and decisions that need follow-up.

# More about `_obsidian/content`

Use:

- `items/social-posts` for LinkedIn, X, and Substack Notes;
- `items/newsletter-issues` for Copy and Context;
- `items/youtube-videos` for YouTube;
- `items/blog-posts` for the Matt Derman blog;
- `ideas` for raw future angles;
- `publications` for channel rules.

Migrated archive material lives in the relevant support folders under `DECLARATION`, `Products`, `Writing`, `Media`, and `Media and Graphics`.
