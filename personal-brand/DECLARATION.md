## Momentum

### Operating Loop

The personal brand runs on visible output.

1. Capture ideas from work, calls, DMs, comments, old posts, drafts, books, and product lessons.
2. Turn one idea into one useful asset.
3. Publish or schedule it.
4. Respond, DM, and learn from the market.
5. Move winners into the next format.
6. Review the queue weekly.

### Accountability Rules

- If nothing shipped, schedule one small post before planning a campaign.
- If a post worked, repurpose it before inventing from scratch.

### Social Selling

#### Method

This is my personal brand method.

LinkedIn is the main platform because it is where the audience, trust, and business intent are strongest.

Test ideas on X. If an idea works on X, expand it into LinkedIn. If LinkedIn works, turn it into YouTube, blog, newsletter, or a lead magnet.

#### Cadence

Use the generated 4-week content schedule plus the daily and weekly periodic notes to plan and review whether the cadence happened. Do not duplicate posting times here.

#### Repurposing Path
Weekly, identify top performers on platforms to waterfall. 

- X winner -> LinkedIn post.
- LinkedIn winner -> YouTube transcript.
- LinkedIn winner -> blog post and newsletter.
- LinkedIn winner -> Copy and Context issue.
- Lead magnet -> carousel/post series.
- YouTube section -> LinkedIn post.
- Newsletter section -> LinkedIn/X/Substack post.

#### Social Selling Routine

60-minute version:

- 5 min: send 30 connection requests.
- 5 min: publish or schedule the post.
- 50 min: DM ICPs, accepted connections, engaged people, profile viewers, and warm relationships.

Daily minimum when active:

- send 40 relevant connection requests;
- DM 10 new prospects from accepted connections in the last 48 hours;
- publish one useful piece of content. Go into DMs then while audience is active.
- follow up with warm conversations.

How to find new connections:
- comments on post
- reactions on post
- profile views (li premium)
- then can also search using e.g. "Founder" OR "Co-Founder" etc.
- when viewing a linkedin profile (lead), click SHOW ALL in the sidebar (more profiles for you)
- additional, use the "people you may know" section as well. 

#### DMs
- Memes, native videos, follow up until actually say no.

#### Where to drive newsletter signups

1. Profile CTA: Copy and Context, Impression, or current lead magnet.
2. Post CTA: soft CTA in the post or first comment.
3. Comment CTA: invite comments around the resource.
4. DM CTA: move warm conversations to subscriber, signup, or feedback call.
5. Newsletter CTA: plug the relevant Impression workflow, product signup, lead magnet, or call.
