# Profile Writing Checklist

Use this checklist after the brand workbook is complete. My profile should make the brand immediately legible to the right person.

## Profile Essentials

- [ ] Profile photo is clear, current, and recognizable.
- [ ] Banner communicates the main promise, audience, or point of view.
- [ ] Headline says who I help, what outcome I create, and how.
- [ ] Featured section points to the most important next step.
- [ ] Contact or CTA path is easy to find.

## Featured Section

Create one-click paths for interested people.

| Asset | Purpose | Link |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Primary offer | Convert qualified buyers | Add current Impression link. |
| Lead magnet or newsletter | Capture warmer audience | Add current lead magnet or Copy and Context link. |
| Proof, case study, or demo | Build trust | Add best proof asset. |
| Best content asset | Show value quickly | Add best LinkedIn GTM asset. |

## About Section

- [ ] Opens with the audience's problem or desired transformation.
- [ ] Explains the product, service, or method in plain language.
- [ ] Includes proof, credibility, or lived experience.
- [ ] Sets expectations for results, process, and who it is for.
- [ ] Ends with a clear CTA.

## Trust Signals

- [ ] Experience is specific and relevant.
- [ ] Testimonials or recommendations are visible.
- [ ] Results are concrete where possible.
- [ ] Past work, clients, projects, or media are linked where useful.

## Brand and Conversion

- [ ] Visual style feels consistent across photo, banner, links, and content.
- [ ] CTA matches the current business priority.
- [ ] The profile reads like it is for one specific person, not everyone.
- [ ] The profile can be understood in under 10 seconds.

## Writing Checklist

- [ ] I use first person where the doc is supposed to sound like me.
- [ ] I avoid generic AI phrasing.
- [ ] I make the audience, problem, method, proof, and CTA clear.
- [ ] I keep strong phrases from my notes when they are better than a cleaned-up rewrite.
- [ ] I separate current answers from older idea dumps.
