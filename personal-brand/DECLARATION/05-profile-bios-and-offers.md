## Short Bio

I am building Impression: LinkedIn GTM software for founders, agencies, and B2B teams who want content to turn into conversations, trust, and revenue.

I write about AI, software, LinkedIn, copywriting, social selling, personal brands, and the messy founder journey of building a company in public.

My stance is simple: useful systems beat productivity theater, taste beats cheap AI slop, and writing is still one of the best ways to think clearly.

## About Section

Two years ago I desperately wanted to start an online business.

Now that business exists, and runs off LinkedIn.

I thought LinkedIn was for cringe corporate quotes, but it is playing social media on easy mode when you learn how to use it properly.

When I first started posting, I literally gave up because it was taking me so long.

That is when I knew what I needed to build.

I am building Impression to help founder-led teams find ideas, write with taste, publish, engage, and turn attention into GTM motion.

I am building it myself, using it myself, and sharing the lessons as the product gets sharper.

AI should increase human agency and creativity. It should not turn smart founders into generic content machines.

CTA: try the product, join the newsletter, download the current lead magnet, or DM me for feedback depending on the campaign.

## Profile Checklist

- [ ] Profile photo is clear, current, and recognizable.
- [ ] Banner communicates the main promise, audience, or point of view.
- [ ] Headline says who I help, what outcome I create, and how.
- [ ] Featured section points to the most important next step.
- [ ] Contact or CTA path is easy to find.
- [ ] About section opens with the audience's problem or desired transformation.
- [ ] About section explains the product, method, proof, and next step.
- [ ] Visual style feels consistent across photo, banner, links, and content.
- [ ] CTA matches the current business priority.
- [ ] The profile reads like it is for one specific person, not everyone.
- [ ] No root profile copy uses old product names.
