# 01 - Define Your Personal Brand

## Final Brand Definition

I help founder-led B2B teams turn LinkedIn into a practical GTM channel with simple, tasteful AI software for content, social selling, and relationship-led growth.

Short version:

> Building Claude Code for LinkedIn GTM.

Current audience:

> early-stage founders who want to grow their linkedin audience and business with inbound leads on linkedin.

I am building Impression for founders, agencies, and B2B teams who want better content, better conversations, and more revenue from LinkedIn.

## Tinder Test

The Tinder Test helps me articulate my message and mission clearly and concisely.

> I help `[person]` get `[what they want]` through `[my vehicle]`.

### Current Draft

I help founder-led B2B teams turn LinkedIn into a practical GTM channel through simple, tasteful AI software for content, social selling, and relationship-led growth.

### Older Drafts

> I help early-stage founders/entrepreneurs grow their brand and gather leads for their business through my actionable, refreshing social media strategies and insights (and app?)

> I help early-stage founders/entrepreneurs grow their online brand and attract leads/clients.

## Core Competence

My core competence is the reason people follow me. I can still write about other topics through my personality pillar, but this is my expertise.

| Prompt                                  | My Answer                                                                                                                        |
| --------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| What do I know better than most people? | Software development, LinkedIn content, AI workflows, personal branding, and building internet businesses from the inside.       |
| What big problem do I solve?            | I help people turn scattered ideas into content, trust, relationships, and leads.                                                |
| What do people ask me for help with?    | Tech, software, personal branding, content, fitness, building online, and figuring out what matters.                             |
| What can I talk about for hours?        | Software, AI, startups, personal brands, social media, writing, fitness, philosophy, and the future of work.                     |
| What transformation changed my life?    | Social media introduced me to fitness, calisthenics, software, online business, and the kind of people who changed my standards. |

Highest-impact competence:

> The topic I know better than most, that excites me, and can be used in the service of other people is founder-led LinkedIn GTM: content, social selling, AI workflows, and personal-brand systems that turn attention into business.

## Niche Formula

A strong niche combines three things:

1. My avatar and desired transformation.
2. My unique method.
3. Social proof or credibility.

| Ingredient | Current Answer |
| --- | --- |
| Avatar and what they get | Founder-led B2B teams get clearer ideas, better LinkedIn posts, better conversations, more trust, and more leads. |
| Unique method | I combine software, AI, audience language, tasteful writing, founder-led storytelling, and social selling. |
| Social proof | I am building the product myself, using it myself, learning from users, and building in public. |

Draft niche statement:

> I help founder-led B2B teams turn LinkedIn from a posting chore into a GTM motion through tasteful AI software, real audience language, and founder-led content systems.

## Method

1. Clarify the audience, offer, and strongest beliefs.
2. Capture real market language from comments, DMs, calls, customers, and old notes.
3. Turn that language into specific content ideas.
4. Write with taste, proof, and a founder point of view.
5. Publish consistently.
6. Use engagement to start better conversations.
7. Feed what works back into the content and product system.

## Proof

- I am building the product myself, using it myself, and learning from users.
- I use it every time I post on LinkedIn.
- I know the problem because I was the market first.
- I had utter conviction in my success, despite any proof of my ability.
- I have always been a builder.
- Social media led me to health, calisthenics, online learning, and software.
- I am building the company in public instead of pretending the strategy arrived fully formed.
