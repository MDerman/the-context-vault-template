# Beliefs

Beliefs to reuse in posts, profile copy, essays, and the Declaration root docs.

> Your brand is an opinion - it should piss some people off.

> Average is over.

> Creator-lead companies will replace established companies that seem impossible to beat.

> You should work to create the opportunity for you to spend as much of your time as possible working on what you believe in.

> Your goal should not be success, money, fame... it should be the opportunity you have created to work on your own stuff.

> Every founder should be lean and strong.

> Think for yourself.

> I'm anti-systems when it comes to personal development, but pro systems big time when it comes to business.

> You can always make something refreshing, inspiring and beautiful on the internet, even if everything seems the same - you just need to work harder.

> If you don't take social media seriously you're doing your future a disservice.

> The more you make other people the more you make yourself.

> The only person worth blaming is yourself.

> Stop planning. Start doing.

> Simplicity.

> What matters can always be boiled down to incredible simplicity.
