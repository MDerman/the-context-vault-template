## Voice Notes

### Sentence Style

- Short when the idea needs force.
- Conversational when replying.
- Specific enough to feel lived.
- Willing to be intense when the truth needs it.

### Favorite Moves

- Name the false default.
- Use a concrete moment.
- Turn personal experience into a useful lesson.
- Contrast taste with cheap AI slop.
- Tie writing, software, and business together.

### Words or Phrases to Reuse

- Average is over.
- Your brand is an opinion.
- The internet can change your life if you take it seriously.
- Taste beats cheap AI slop.
- Writing is thinking.
- Build proof before claiming authority.

### Words or Phrases to Avoid

- Generic founder advice.
- Over-polished AI phrasing.
- Vague "personal brand" language without proof.
- Old product names.
