# 03-business Declaration

## Identity

### Purpose

Impression is the B2B social selling platform for creators, founders, and teams.

It helps users turn social content into business outcomes: better ideas, better posts, smarter repurposing, cleaner scheduling, useful analytics, and stronger conversion from engagement into relationships and revenue.

The vision: Impression becomes the de-facto B2B social selling platform for creators and solo-founders, and later employee advocacy teams.

### Team Values

- Every Day is Day 1.
- Make Magic possible.
- Automate, eliminate, delegate.
- Invent and wander.
- Optimize for flow, human agency and creativity
